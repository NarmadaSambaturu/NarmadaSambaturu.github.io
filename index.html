Research Interests

Computational Biology, Systems Biology, Epidemiology, Vaccines and Immunotherapy, Multi-scale Modelling, Immune Response, Cancer Genomics, Host-pathogen Interactions.

I am interested in developing methods to solve biological questions both at a fundamental and a translational level. Some of the questions I am interested in are:

  • - Studying the mechanism of action of any perturbation, including drugs, pathogens or tumors, and the response of the host to these perturbations.

  • - Analysing disease spread in a population, and making epidemic models more biologically accurate.

  • - Understanding the interplay between tumor cells and their microenvironment, including immune response and the mechanisms used by the tumor to evade it; studying the evolution of heterogeneous tumor cells in this complex environment.

  • - Stratification of patients, and matching them with the most appropriate therapy, including potential combination therapies.

  • - Tackling emerging resistance to therapy, or recurrence of disease.

  • - Rational vaccine design.
